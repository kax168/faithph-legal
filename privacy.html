Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 06, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes the policies and procedures of FaithPH ("the App", "We", "Us", or "Our") regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of Your information when You use the App. By using FaithPH, You agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When You sign in to FaithPH using Google Sign-In or Apple Sign-In, We may collect the following information provided by the authentication provider:

  • Display name (first name and/or last name)
  • Email address
  • Profile photo URL
✅ We do NOT collect:
Phone number · Physical address or location (GPS) · Contacts or phone book · Camera or photo library · Financial or payment information (at this time)

User-Generated Content

FaithPH allows You to create prayer journal entries. This content is stored locally on Your Device by default, associated with Your account if signed in, and never shared with third parties.

Usage Data

We may collect limited usage data automatically through Firebase, including device type, operating system version, app usage patterns, crash reports, and performance data. This data helps us improve the App's stability and performance.

3. Local Notifications

FaithPH may send You daily verse reminder notifications if You enable this feature. Notification permissions are requested through Your Device's standard permission system. You can enable or disable notifications at any time through the App's Settings or Your Device's system settings.

4. How We Use Your Data

We use Your Personal Data for the following purposes:

  • To provide the App: Display Your profile information and manage Your account.
  • To personalize Your experience: Save favorites and prayer journal entries.
  • To send notifications: Deliver daily Bible verse reminders when You opt in.
  • To improve the App: Use anonymous usage data to understand and improve features.
✅ We do NOT use Your data for:
Advertising or marketing · Selling to third parties · Profiling or automated decision-making

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent Your Personal Data to third parties. We may share Your information only:

  • With Firebase (Google): Solely for authentication and app analytics.
  • For legal requirements: If required by law or valid legal requests.
  • With Your consent: For other purposes only with Your explicit consent.

6. Data Storage & Security

  • Prayer journal entries and favorites are stored locally on Your Device.
  • Authentication data is managed by Firebase on Google's secure servers.
  • We implement commercially reasonable security measures, but no electronic storage is 100% secure.

7. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained for the duration of Your account.
  • Local data: Remains on Your Device until You clear it using "Clear All Data" in Settings.
  • Usage data: Anonymous analytics may be retained for up to 24 months.

8. Your Rights — Delete Your Data

You have the right to delete Your Personal Data at any time:

  • In-App: Use "Clear All Data" in Settings to remove all local data.
  • Sign Out: Disconnect Your Google or Apple account.
  • Contact Us: Email us to request deletion of any data We hold.

9. Children's Privacy

FaithPH provides Bible verses and prayer journaling features suitable for users of all ages. However, We do not knowingly collect Personal Data from children under 13 without parental consent. If You believe a child has provided Us with Personal Data, please contact Us.

10. Changes to This Policy
